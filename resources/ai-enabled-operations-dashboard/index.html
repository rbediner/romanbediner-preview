AI-Enabled Operations Dashboard

An interactive, single-screen dashboard prototype for AI-enabled operating visibility, structured execution, and faster review in modern AI-enabled work.

Designed as a shareable operating artifact, this dashboard prototype brings demand, delivery, financial tension, customer pressure, and AI leverage into a single-screen view. It offers a structured way to review what matters, spot pressure quickly, and make decisions with less translation across tools and reports.

What This Dashboard Helps Answer

  • Are we creating enough demand?
  • Are we closing the right business?
  • Are we delivering efficiently?
  • Are we improving operating margin?
  • Is customer friction increasing?
  • Is AI creating real leverage?
  • What needs attention right now?

In This Dashboard

Demand / Commercial

Left-side zones track opportunity flow, conversion quality, and revenue pressure signals.

Core Truth / Center Lens

The center module anchors the current operating truth through Margin, Revenue, and AI views.

Delivery / Operations

Right-side zones surface utilization, onboarding velocity, and delivery risk as service pressure rises.

Targets / Action Signals

The bottom strip keeps target context and the immediate operating action visible in one line.

Core Dashboard Views

Margin
Shows current operating margin, target, gap, trend, and the primary driver affecting performance.

Revenue
Shows current revenue truth or forecast, target, gap, trend, and the commercial driver affecting results.

AI
Shows whether AI is producing measurable leverage, how far that is from target, and what is driving adoption or drag.
