AI-Enabled Operations Dashboard

An interactive, single-screen dashboard prototype for AI-enabled operating visibility, structured execution, and faster review in modern AI-enabled work.

View Source Code →

Designed as a shareable operating artifact, this dashboard prototype brings demand, delivery, financial tension, customer pressure, and AI leverage into a single-screen view. It offers a structured way to review what matters, spot pressure quickly, and make decisions with less translation across tools and reports.

What This Dashboard Helps Answer

  • Are we creating enough demand?
  • Are we closing the right business?
  • Are we delivering efficiently?
  • Are we improving operating margin?
  • Is customer friction increasing?
  • Is AI creating real leverage?
  • What needs attention right now?

In This Dashboard

The layout follows one fixed business narrative.

  • Left shows demand generation and commercial quality. It answers whether the pipeline is healthy, conversion is moving, and revenue forecast is holding.
  • The center shows the core truth of the business right now. This is the primary focal point and rotates between Margin, Revenue, and AI.
  • Right shows delivery efficiency and operational risk. It surfaces capacity pressure, onboarding friction, service health, and cycle time.
  • Bottom shows target context and the current action signal. It tells leadership what target matters and what should happen next.

This interactive dashboard is designed for TV, desktop, or tablet review. For the full experience, please switch to a larger screen.

Core Dashboard Views

Margin
Shows current operating margin, target, gap, trend, and the primary driver affecting performance.

Revenue
Shows current revenue truth or forecast, target, gap, trend, and the commercial driver affecting results.

AI
Shows whether AI is producing measurable leverage, how far that is from target, and what is driving adoption or drag.

Operating Principles

  • One screen, one story
  • Numeric first, not status-language first
  • Show tension without clutter
  • Keep the center as the truth anchor
  • Treat AI as an operating signal, not a novelty
  • Make customer and service pressure visible as part of margin pressure

The source code is available in a dedicated standalone repository so this artifact can be reviewed, adapted, downloaded, or packaged with repository tools such as Repomix without pulling the full site codebase.
