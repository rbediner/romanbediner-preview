THE ENTERPRISE SCALING DISNEY & GLOBAL DTC

The foundation of this operating philosophy rests on the belief that scale breaks things that previously worked. Leadership focus has centered on stepping into high-growth environments where execution has stalled, and re-architecting the underlying systems - not just the technology, but the operating models, decision frameworks, and cross-functional agreements that turn strategy into reality.

As digital adoption became standard, transformation advanced into global streaming platforms. Leadership roles at Amazon and later at The Walt Disney Company centered on Quality of Experience, performance engineering, and operational readiness across Disney+, ESPN+, and major live streaming events. During this period, device validation constraints across distributed teams led to the architecture of the AI-Enabled Remote Device Control and Automation System, eliminating physical bottlenecks and embedding structured, AI-assisted device control directly into operational telemetry. The system received internal recognition for architectural innovation.

THE HIGH-GROWTH STARTUP ACQUISITION & SCALE

Early operating leadership developed during a structural shift in media and sports distribution. Live leaderboards, real-time shot tracking, streaming applications, and digital performance platforms expanded rapidly across organizations including PGA TOUR, Miami Heat, and NASCAR. Traditional broadcast models converged with digital distribution, requiring coordinated product, engineering, and content delivery systems capable of supporting national audiences at scale.

Execution structure became the stabilizing force in high-velocity environments. Product innovation translated into scalable release discipline, performance telemetry, cross-functional alignment, and measurable delivery cadence. Growth demanded operating systems capable of absorbing rapid adoption without fragmentation across teams, tools, vendors, and decision layers, ensuring digital platforms scaled with reliability rather than improvisation.

THE ADVISORY & PARTNERSHIP EMBEDDED EXECUTIVE LEADERSHIP

Embedded operating authority across a next-generation global data transport platform integrating subsea cable systems, terrestrial fiber, satellite infrastructure, and AI-based monitoring and predictive modeling of atmospheric and network performance conditions. Operating design governs how regional P&L entities interface, how commercial pipeline converts to deployable network capacity, and how execution aligns across jurisdictions, vendors, and regulatory environments.

Enterprise backbone capabilities anchor infrastructure scale, including CRM and revenue operations, Configure-Price-Quote (CPQ) and end-to-end Order-to-Cash architecture, Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) and financial controls, and Business Support Systems (BSS) and Operational Support Systems (OSS) coordinating commercial, provisioning, and network operations. A globally distributed support organization functions with defined intake, escalation, and service-level ownership, reinforcing disciplined execution across capital-intensive infrastructure programs.

Operating Philosophy

Systems Over Heroics

Operational success is a function of design, not individual effort. High-performing organizations rely on repeatable mechanisms and clear telemetry rather than heroic intervention to maintain velocity.

  • Established delivery cadences that reduce cross-team friction
  • Strengthened prioritization using structured operational telemetry
  • Governance boundaries remain explicit while automation increases
  • Structured signal detection improves decision velocity

Embedded Operating Leadership

This is not about advising from the sidelines. It is about embedding operational discipline directly into workflows, ensuring that delivery commitments are met reliably and strategy is translated into daily execution.

  • Embedded operating leadership during high-growth or infrastructure buildout
  • Executive advisory focused on operating architecture and governance
  • Operator coaching for senior leaders navigating expanded scope
  • Cross-functional program alignment in high-visibility initiatives
