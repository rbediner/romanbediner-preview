How we might connect

If something on this site resonated, feel free to reach out. Many conversations start with a simple exchange of ideas rather than a defined engagement.

People usually connect with me around a few themes:

  • Exploring operational challenges in product, engineering, or platform organizations
  • Comparing notes on scaling execution in high-growth environments
  • Leadership conversations with operators navigating growth or career transitions
  • Execution leadership coaching for operators and emerging leaders
  • Brainstorming ideas, exchanging perspectives, or connecting across networks

Sometimes the best next step is simply a thoughtful conversation. If you are exploring an idea, navigating a complex situation, or just want to compare notes with another operator, I am always open to a quick exchange.
